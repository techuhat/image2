Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how ImagePDF Toolkit protects your data with 100% client-side processing.

Privacy-First Approach

ImagePDF Toolkit is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring your files and data never leave your device.

100% Client-Side Processing

All image and PDF processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

No Data Storage

We do not store, collect, or retain any of your files or personal information. Once you close the browser tab, all data is gone.

No Tracking or Analytics

We don't use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics, or any other monitoring technologies. Your browsing behavior is completely private.

Open Source Transparency

Our code is open source and available on GitHub. You can review exactly how the tools work and verify our privacy claims.

Third-Party Services

This website uses minimal third-party resources:

  • Fonts: We may load fonts from Google Fonts for better typography. This is the only external resource loaded.
  • Hosting: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect basic server logs as per GitHub's privacy policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us at techuhat@gmail.com.
